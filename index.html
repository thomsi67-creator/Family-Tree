Contribute a story or photo

If you have a story, a memory, a scanned document, or photos you'd like included, you can send them in. Please avoid sharing sensitive details about living people publicly - we'll handle those privately.

  • Preferred: use Request Access so we can verify you, then we'll share the best way to contribute.
  • Quick send: email myfamilytree12@outlook.com (add who/where/when if you can).
  • Or add a short note to a story page: Stories / Gallery.
